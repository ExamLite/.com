About us

I started this website in late 2023 as a way to learn WebDev in my free time. If only I knew how time consuming this would be before I started.

Ultimately, my goal is to provide a free source of study material for anyone who needs it. No catch. No logins. No subscriptions.

CompTIA sells study guides in the upwards of $1000. It is their exam and they have every right to do so. Lets be honest, most people who are trying to get their Sec+ cert can't afford to drop that kind of money on a study guide package. Unfortunately, having the Sec+ cert is a pre-requisite for getting your foot in the door at a lot of places now. I don't think your financial status should hold you back from progressing in your career.

Whether you're a broke kid looking to make a career change; A military member transitioning into the civilian world; Some other 3rd thing! I invite you to use this site as a free resource to work toward your certs. Bear with me as I prepare this website to provide you with everything you need to pass.
